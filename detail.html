Exclusive: Mahesh to venture into the food business

Superstar Mahesh Babu is currently enjoying his foreign trip with his family. He is expected to return to Hyderabad soon.

  • Filmy Focus, Publish Date - July 10, 2022 11:57 IST
Image Credit: PTI

Superstar Mahesh Babu is currently enjoying his foreign trip with his family. He is expected to return to Hyderabad soon.

Meanwhile, the latest we hear is that Mahesh is all set to venture into the restaurant business. Reportedly, he will be collaborating with Minerva groups of hotels and establishing a restaurant at Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad.

It is widely known that Mahesh is already into to the Multiplexes screening sector and launched AMB Cinemas at Gachibowli in association with Asian Cinemas.
